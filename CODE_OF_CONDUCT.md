# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in GuardianStar a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Give feedback on code, not on people.
- Share context when reporting bugs or proposing changes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insults, or sustained disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or edit comments, issues, or pull requests that violate this Code of Conduct and may restrict participation when necessary.

## Scope

This policy applies within repository spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and review comments.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact maintainers privately and include as much detail as possible.
